Perth Scorchers

Perth, Australia

Team Information:

The Perth Scorchers is an Australian domestic Twenty20 franchise cricket team representing Perth in the Big Bash League (BBL). The Scorchers are the equal-most successful team in BBL history, tied with the Sydney Sixers, winning three championships and coming runners up three times. They were defeated in their second final by the Brisbane Heat in BBL02. They then won the next two consecutive championships, becoming the first team to achieve the feat in the league's history. These wins came against the Hobart Hurricanes and the Sydney Sixers in a last-ball thriller at Canberra's Manuka Oval.

The Scorchers also have a side in the Women's Big Bash League.
